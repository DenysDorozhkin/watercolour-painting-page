About birds

There’s a quiet power in watching birds take to the sky. Their flight feels effortless, unchained — a natural expression of freedom. They don’t struggle or hesitate; they simply rise. In their graceful motion, we’re reminded that true freedom is often quiet, instinctive, and found in being exactly what we’re meant to be.
